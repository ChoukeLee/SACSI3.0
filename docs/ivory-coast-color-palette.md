## Design Notes

- Use orange as the main interaction accent, not as a full-page dominant background.
- Use green for positive operational states: available, paid, healthy, completed.
- Keep white and warm paper surfaces dominant so the app remains professional and readable.
- Do not rely on color alone for room status. Pair color with label, border, or icon.
